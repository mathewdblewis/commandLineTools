1.5 teaspoons salt
1.5 teaspoons yeast
1.5 cups water
2 cups flour

put salt and yeast in bowl and pour warm water on it then add flour and stir it
let rest for half hour
take out of bowl, flour it and fold a few times
put in oiled bowl and rise for 1 hour
cut into 3 peices and rise for half hour
cook at 480F for 35 minutes
