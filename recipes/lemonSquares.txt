modified from: allrecipes.com/recipe/10294/the-best-lemon-bars


0.75 cup butter, softened
0.33 cup white sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1.50 cups all-purpose flour
8 eggs
2 cups white sugar
0.50 cup all-purpose flour
5 lemons, juice and zest

preheat oven to 350 F
mix butter 1.5 cups flour and .33 cup sugar and vanilla, put in the bottom of pan
bake crust 15 minutes
wisk 2 cups sugar, 0.50 cup flour, eggs, lemon juice and pour over crust
bake 25 minutes
put in fridge until cold
