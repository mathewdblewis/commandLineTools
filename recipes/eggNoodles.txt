2-2.5 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt per egg (3 teaspoons)
3 eggs

beat the eggs then put them into a well of salted flour, then mix and kneed
roll thin then trim to make the dough rectangular, then cut strips
to cut the strips, roll the dough up then cut it then unroll the noodles
you can store them for a few months in the freezer in an air tight container
to cook them, bring water to boil then cook for 7-10 minutes
