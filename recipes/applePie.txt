Engredients:
3/4 cup sugar
3 Tablespoons flour
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
9-10 cups apples (cut into 3/4 inch think slices)
egg wash

Instructions:
1. Combine sugar, flour, and spices in a large bowl.
2. Peel, quarter & core apples. Cut into 3/4-inch slices.
3. Stir apples into the flour mixture and place in prepared pie pan.
4. Roll top pastry between floured wax paper and place on top. Trim or flute edges.
5. Brush top with egg wash
6. Poke about 12 slits in the top using a sharp knife.
   Cut out 1/2-inch circle in the center to release steam.
   Place on baking sheet in case of spills.
7. Bake for 50-55 minutes until crust is golden
