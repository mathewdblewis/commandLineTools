Tang Zhong:
1/4 cup Flour
3/4 cup Water

Milk Bread:
2+2/3 cup Flour
1/2 teaspoon Salt
2 tablespoons Sugar
1+1/4 teaspoon Yeast
1 Egg
1/2 cup lukewarm milk
Tang Zhong
3 tablespoons Butter

microwave butter and milk to form liquid then add sugar salt and egg
once homogenous and luke warm, add yeast
once yeast disolves, add tangzhong then add flour
then stir then knead

rise for 45 minutes
shape for cooking
rise for 30 minutes
cook for 25 minutes at 350
