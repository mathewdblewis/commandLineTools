Ingredient Checklist
2 cups maple syrup
1 cup heavy cream
¾ cup dark brown sugar
2 tablespoons butter
1 pinch salt
⅔ cup chopped walnuts
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with waxed paper and butter the paper
Boil maple syrup, heavy cream, and brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat
Continue to boil, without stirring, until mixture starts to bubble and registers
240 degrees F (166 degrees C) on a candy thermometer then remove from heat
Add butter and salt but do not mix
Let sit until mixture reaches 105 degs F
Add walnuts and vanilla and beat until mixture loses its gloss
Pour into the prepared pan and let cool to room temperature
Cut, wrap, and store in fridge
